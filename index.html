Biography

Dr Chiew-Foong “C.F.” Kwong is an academic leader in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, currently serving as Head of Department and Associate Professor at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China. He leads the Municipal Key Discipline (Peak Discipline), supported by significant government investment.

His research spans mobile communications, C-V2X, IoT, satellite navigation, and AI, with a focus on integrating 5G/6G systems into intelligent transportation and smart cities.

He is a Chartered Engineer (UK), Senior Member of IEEE, Member of IET, and Senior Fellow of Advance HE. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
